Post Traumatic Amnesia App

This application facilitates easy assessment of Post Traumatic Amnesia, a condition that occurs after a person suffers a traumatic brain injury.

Currently in order to evaluate the presence of this condition, a therapist asks the patient suffering from it a series of routine questions after which the therapist makes a note of how the patient performed i.e. the number of correct answers.

The objective of building this application was to ease in the assessment process of Post Traumatic Amnesia by making it available to medical facilities at which they have brain injury patients. So you can fork or download the project from here and evaluate it for yourself to see if the app fulfils your need.
